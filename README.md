For the final project for this course, I worked on a program that used machine learning to solve a maze called TreasureMaze. I wrote the main game loop where the agent made actions and improved through repetition. 

## What do computer scientists do and why does it matter?
Computer scientists like myself study computers and their algorithms, and design software, hardware, and other technologies for computers. They matter because the technologies they work on are powerful and fuel our modern world. They world to solve complex problems that are then used across industries.

## How do I approach a problem as a computer scientist?
I try to always approach problems with as much information as possible and look for patterns. When trying to solve problems I break up issues into easily solved pieces and write up plans before I execute them. When I am trying to implement solutions, I frequently test to make sure I am not wasting effort or developing ineffective solutions.

## What are my ethical responsibilities to the end user and the organization?
I believe that I am responsible to the end user's safety and privacy, and to make solutions that avoid bias or discrimination against users. My work can shape society so it is critical that I act with integrity. For my organization I must act honestly and with my own independent ethical judgments in order to be responsible and act in accordance with my duties.
